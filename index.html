A deeper therapeutic process

Therapy that connects past patterns to present change.

We often underestimate how past experiences influence our personality, self-image, coping strategies, and current relationship patterns. Therapy can help bridge the gaps between the past and present so you can better understand yourself, your relationships, and the patterns that no longer serve you.

“Therapy isn’t truly transformative if it doesn’t challenge you.”

Dr. Poster’s approach is non-judgmental, empathetic, honest, and collaborative, with moments of humor for relief. The goal is to build a strong therapeutic relationship and supportive foundation to safely explore uncomfortable thoughts and feelings.

Clinical background

Specialized experience with complex clinical and forensic settings.

Dr. Poster earned her Ph.D. from The New School in New York City. Her training included work at the Center for the Intensive Treatment of Personality Disorders, Mount Sinai, and Nassau University Medical Center.

Before private practice, she spent nearly ten years working as a clinical and forensic psychologist for the New York Police Department and the U.S. Department of Justice.

Her areas of expertise include relational functioning and personality dynamics, attachment theory, diagnostic assessment, and mental health support for first responders. She has specialized training in pre-employment and fitness-for-duty evaluations, perinatal mental health, and Dialectical Behavior Therapy.

First responders & veterans

Specialized support for people who serve.

Dr. Poster’s background includes nearly a decade of clinical and forensic psychology work with the New York Police Department and the U.S. Department of Justice.

This experience informs her work with first responders, veterans, and professionals navigating stress, trauma exposure, occupational demands, burnout, relationship strain, and the psychological impact of service-oriented work.

Diagnostic & forensic expertise

Clinical insight with assessment experience.

Dr. Poster has specialized training in diagnostic assessment, pre-employment evaluations, fitness-for-duty evaluations, personality dynamics, and forensic psychology.

Her clinical style balances warmth and honesty, helping clients feel supported while also being challenged toward meaningful growth.

Telehealth services

Secure virtual care with broad state access.
